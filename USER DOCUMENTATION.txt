Introduction:

Welcome to the Affinity Blog Application User Documentation. This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively.
Getting Started:

Registration:
To access the blog application, you don't need to register an account, but to be able to post you need one
Click on the "Sign Up" button on top right of the navigation menu
Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and password.
Click "Sign Up" to create your account.

Login:
After registering, you can log in to the application.
Enter your email address and password.
Click "Login" to access your account.

Dashboard:
Upon successful login, you will be directed to the dashboard.
The dashboard provides an overview of your blog posts and account information.

Creating a New Blog Post:
To create a new blog post, follow these steps:
Click on the "New Post" button on the dashboard or in the navigation menu.
Fill in the required details, such as the title, content, and category of your blog post.
Optionally, you can upload an image to accompany your post.
Click "Save" or "Publish" to create and publish your blog post.

Editing a Blog Post:
To edit an existing blog post, follow these steps:
Go to the dashboard and locate the blog post you want to edit.
Click on the "Edit" button or the blog post title to open the editing interface.
Make the desired changes to the title, content, category, or image.
Click "Save" to update the changes.

Deleting a Blog Post:
To delete a blog post, follow these steps:
Go to the dashboard and locate the blog post you want to delete.
Click on the "Delete" button or the blog post title.
Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Uploading Images:
When creating or editing a blog post, you can upload an image to accompany your post.
Click on the "Upload Image" button in the image section.
Select the image file from your computer.
Wait for the image to upload and display a preview.
Click "Save" to attach the image to your blog post.

Searching for Blog Posts:
To search for specific blog posts, use the search functionality.
Locate the search bar, typically located in the sidebar.
Enter keywords related to the blog post you're looking for.
Press "Enter" or click on the search button.
The application will display a list of matching blog posts.

Logout:
To log out of the application, click on "Logout" button in the navigation menu.
